## Decisions Made

- Mastery dot colors follow RESEARCH.md guidance: `onSurfaceVariant` (grey) for new, `brandOrange` for learning, `success` (#34C759) for mastered
- Word detail does NOT reuse `ClozeCardDisplay` — avoids importing challenge mechanics into a data-display screen (RESEARCH.md Pitfall 5)
- `focusKey` integer pattern: a single incrementing integer as useMemo dependency drives full recompute of both `masteryMap` and `chapterMastery` on every screen focus
- `getChapterMastery` called per-chapter inside the `chapterMastery` memo (co-located with `focusKey`) rather than inside `renderSectionHeader` to keep rendering pure
